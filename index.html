What's involved?

Watch the video and review the 3 steps below to see if you can participate.

Step 1

You will take a short online questionnaire about your health and your medicines. You will need your blood pressure and cholesterol numbers from within the past 12 months.

Step 2

After taking the questionnaire, you may have the opportunity to purchase the medicine.

Step 3

After purchasing, you will have a "virtual" visit to confirm participation. If after Visit 1, you are not eligible to participate, the product purchase price will be refunded.

Who may be able to take part?

  • People interested in cholesterol or heart health
  • Men and Women aged 20-75
  • Able to read, speak, and understand English

What you need

To complete Part 1, you will need health numbers:

Blood Pressure

Cholesterol Levels

Total Cholesterol, HDL and LDL Cholesterol, Triglycerides.

How to get your cholesterol levels

You can check your recent lab report (from within the past 12 months) or get them from your doctor or patient portal. They will often appear like this.

If you don't have a recent report or a doctor, there are low-cost options that do not require a doctor visit:

  1. Home-use test kits
  2. Heart health screenings
  3. Some pharmacies
  4. Employer health clinics

If you need assistance with reading your lab report, talk to a doctor or call (317) 705-2187
